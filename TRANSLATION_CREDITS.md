# Translation sources and credits

WATI Core imports only short content display names into its fallback
catalog. It does not redistribute the supplied `.lang` files. At runtime,
consumers should prefer the localization key from the original resource pack;
WATI's own value is used only when that key is unavailable.

## Spanish (Mexico)

- AresgettaYT: compilation and the translations identified as Ares/Mapple
  Server translations in the supplied packs.
- HonKit26113: editing of the Beyond The Underground language source;
  translation credited in that source to Ares for Mapple Server.
- Polarin: translation credit present in End's Delight Bedrock and Nether's
  Delight Bedrock.
- _.xrichardx._: translation assistance credit present in Farmer's Delight
  Bedrock.
- Other source-pack authors and translators retain credit for names originating
  in their projects.

Better On Bedrock and SplitBlocks are intentionally marked **source-only**.
Their Spanish strings are not copied into WATI. When those addons are active,
their own resource packs provide the visible names.

The machine-readable import decisions and coverage are recorded in
`registry/translation_import_report.json`.
